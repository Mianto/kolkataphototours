Photo-Tours and Photo-Walks


Photo-Walks

Visiting a new location for photography is intimidating and it comes with own difficulties. Specially with country like India, where there are 122 major languages have its own challenges. If you get to connect with a local person of that particular place, things simplifies a lot. I have been wandering in the alleys of kolkata from last 8 years, So I think I know my city inside-out. In Photo-walks, I'll be taking you to different locations for shooting some amazing ongoing emotions.

At the end of the photo-walk, it's my promise that you will be having images that you can cherish for the rest of your life.

Assistant Photography

If you're in Kolkata for your photography or film-making project, I would love to assist you. I am also a part of photography world so I have understandings of how things work with photographers and having me in your back-pack during your project-shoot can reduce a lot of work. Being local, I'll be approching and handling people to make sure that your shoot went as per your your wish. It will be awesome to carry your bucket of Gears and KFC Chicken during the shoot

I can assure you that you'll have an awesome experience to work in the city of Joy that you can share with whole world.

Photo-Tours

If you are planning to visit Kolkata to photograph some of the iconic locations, I'll help you to find the best spots to take the iconic shots and get you an insider journey across the kolkata taking you to your desired location and also connect to the local people to get access to photograph the culture and ethnicity of the people of Kolkata.

Workshops

After doing two years of constant photography and eight years of regular walk in this city, I have realised that there is a lot to learn on and off the camera. This city is full of beautiful people, mesmerising culture and some amazing colorful streets. As a part of the Workshop, I'll be telling all about how to work in this city and how to approach and deal with the people before you take their photos. The basic composition of the street photography, lights in potraits, capturing emotions and much more are a part of this workshop.

I'll assure you take amazing photos from your holiday and gain skills that will stay with you long back.
