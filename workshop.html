Workshops

After doing two years of constant photography and eight years of regular walk in this city, I have realised that there is a lot to learn on and off the camera. This city is full of beautiful people, mesmerising culture and some amazing colorful streets. As a part of the Workshop, I'll be telling all about how to work in this city and how to approach and deal with the people before you take their photos. The basic composition of the street photography, lights in potraits, capturing emotions and much more are a part of this workshop.

I'll assure you take amazing photos from your holiday and gain skills that will stay with you long back.
